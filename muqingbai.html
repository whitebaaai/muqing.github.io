Born and raised in a Manchurian family, I have always been fascinated by the dialogue between nature and the diverse cultures it raises.

I hold a Master of Design Studies from Harvard Graduate School of Design and a Bachelor of Architecture from the Architectural Association. I am excited about designs that mediate human-ecological conflicts. I believe in mediums that leverage the power of symbiosis between humans and nature. Therefore, I have developed my academic interests at the intersection of biological material applications and digital healthcare to contribute to the equilibrium of human-ecological well-beings.

I enjoy interacting with people and solving challenging problems, so I look forward to collaborating with those who share similar goals and values!
