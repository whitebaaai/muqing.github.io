Immersive Soundscape

Head-controlled multisensory experience

Immersive Soundscape is a wearable device that creates multi-dimensional audio environments. Accelerometer & water atomization sensors are integrated into the wearable device to modify audio with motion & to trigger scent vapor release.

The immersive experience aims to create an interactive and motion-active process of sound making, where users listen and create variations of the same music. Usersâ€™ head movements would trigger changes in pitch, playing speed and additional sound effects.
