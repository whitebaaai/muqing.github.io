Diet I/O

Just-in-time nudge for changes in dietary decision-making

Diet I/O leverages real-time health data and AI-based image processing models to create users with just-in-time reflections for behavioral change. It is a perceptually “misleading” strategy that aims to encourage the users to make informed decisions by showing the possible consequences of accumulated behavioral patterns on the well-being of both the users and the environments in which they reside. Given the ability to foresee an unexpected vision based on activities that people are often unconscious of, users are expected to pause and re-evaluate potential damages to their health and the places they belong to. Achieving user’s reactions is a critical measure of success for educating the larger population about the symbiotic relationship between humans and the environment..
