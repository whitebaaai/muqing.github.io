Memini

A GenAI-powered platform to empower seniors to capture life stories and forge meaningful connections.

Memini; "I remember" in Latin, was born out of the Againg Track as the 1st-place Winnder at the MIT HackingMedicine in April 2024.

It is currently an ongoing stealth-mode startup project that aims to celebrate the life experiences of seniors with memory loss and loneliness.

Business Model

Memini will start as a direct-to-consumer service for users to trial the platform. As part of Memini’s growth, our goal is to do outcomes-based contracting with insurance companies. And separately, we would like to expand our direct-to-consumer model by incorporating a monthly subscription fee. Our customer acquisition strategy includes a mix of strategic and channel partnerships, including genealogy and genetic testing companies who value our data.
